AI Business Idea Report

AI-Powered Tutoring Systems can revolutionize education in Jamaica by providing personalized learning support to students across the island.Teachers usually have to give the "same lesson" to everyone, even though students have different learning styles, strengths, and limitations. An adaptive learning system can be applied by creating customized exercises for each student based on their needs, learning pace, and preference for visual, auditory, or drill-based activities. It can also dynamically stimulate weak areas.

Why it is Useful

  • Learning that is more efficient involves focusing on what you need rather than what you already know.
  • Seeing development personalized to your needs will increase your confidence.
  • Fills in the gaps where certain students can fall behind due to variations in style or pace.

    • Ethical Concerns

    These include bias in training data, over-reliance on technology, data privacy (because the system gathers performance data), and unequal access for pupils without devices or internet. My idea is to assure strong data privacy, fairness audits, and low-cost or offline alternatives to name a few.
